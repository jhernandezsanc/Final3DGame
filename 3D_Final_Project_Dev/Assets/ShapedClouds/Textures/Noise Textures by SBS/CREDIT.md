Hand picked noise textures from "700+ Noise Textures" by Screaming Brain Studios, licensed under CC0.

Link to the pack: https://opengameart.org/content/700-noise-textures

Link to their website: https://screamingbrainstudios.com/downloads/

Highly recommended to download it if you want more textures than the included ones.

They've all already been setup the ideal way for this tool in Unity, single channel texture with no compression, limited to 512x512 size (though none of those textures goes beyond that) and renamed to be easier to use and identify - the SBS prefix refers to the author, Screaming Brain Studios, and ensures they're all grouped together when selecting a texture.

These textures were used for the images in the store, which are screenshots from the included examples.
You can replicate their exact appearance out of the box.

You DON'T need to credit the author when using these textures if you don't want to or can't.
No textures with licenses other than CC0 were included to avoid accidental copyright infringements on the part of the user.
I'm sure they'd appreciate if you did, regardless.

However, I encourage you to look for better or more unique textures, even at the cost of a more restrictive license.
Clouds work best when they have a unique appearance, even if they're realistic.

Troubleshooting

If clouds aren't rendering or are rendering incorrectly, here's a checklist of simple things that can cause issues:

  • Not using URP/using Unity 2021 or older.
    • Causes compilation errors, certain features are simply not available and the asset won't work.
  • Depth texture isn't enabled in the Pipeline Asset.
    • Causes clouds to always render on top of everything.
    • NOT required if Render Above Opaques is disabled in the Clouds Controller.
  • Tonemapping/post processing is disabled.
    • This asset was made with HDR and tonemapping in mind (untested with HDR displays).
    • Either enable tonemapping or tune settings to lower brightness.
      • The most direct way of doing that is by changing the color of the clouds to a darker tone.
  • Final resolution is too high.
    • Causes a significant performance impact.
    • Usually using half is not noticeable and significantly faster.
  • Base resolution is too high.
    • Causes a significant performance impact.
    • For the base resolution, a very low resolution is usually acceptable, as the detail is added by the cloud filter.
    • The asset can produce good looking clouds really fast because this step is almost free at low enough resolutions.

If you checked everything here and still had issues, please contact the developer and explain what happened in as much detail as possible.
